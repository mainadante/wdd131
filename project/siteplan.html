Global Travel Explorer - Site Plan

Logo

This name was chosen because it reflects the website's purpose of helping users explore travel destinations around the world. The word "Global" emphasizes the international focus of the site, while "Travel Explorer" highlights the opportunity for visitors to discover destinations, attractions, restaurants, hotels, and activities. The name is memorable, relevant to travelers, and clearly communicates the website's goal.

Optional Domain Availability: globaltravelexplorer.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of Global Travel Explorer is to help travelers discover and plan trips to destinations around the world. The site provides destination guides, information about popular tourist attractions, recommended hotels and restaurants, local activities, travel tips, and tools that allow users to filter destinations and save their favorite places for future travel planning.

Scenarios

  • What are the top tourist attractions to visit in my chosen destination?
  • Which hotels and restaurants are recommended for my budget and travel preferences?
  • What local activities and experiences are available at this destination?

Color Schema

  • Burnt Orange (#D94B1F): Used for buttons, call-to-action elements, and important highlights. This adds energy and excitement to the site.
  • Deep Navy (#0F172A): Used for body text and readability. This keeps content clear and professional while still modern.

Typography

  • Heading Font: "Shadows Into Light" - A handwritten, casual font with a lot of personality.
  • Body Font: "Elms Sans" - A versatile sans-serif font that is highly legible, used for body text and descriptions.

Wireframes

Mobile Wireframe

A simple single-column layout designed for mobile devices. Content is stacked vertically, allowing users to easily browse destinations, travel tips, favorites, and contact information through scrolling.

Desktop Wireframe

A wider layout designed for larger screens. Navigation is displayed horizontally, and content sections such as featured destinations are arranged side-by-side to make better use of available space and improve the user experience.
